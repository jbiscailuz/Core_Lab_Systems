About

I decided to concentrate on the importance of embedding the technology into garments as explained in Maureen Laverty's article. I focused on the idea of process instead of final result. What choices does one make when creating wearable technology? How to hide or expose the different aspects of the technology within the garment? I decided to mix these two elements by using only technological graphics but manipulating them to the point where they become something else completely. By adding different textures it gave the piece more dimension and movement despite it being static, representing the embedded technology working.
